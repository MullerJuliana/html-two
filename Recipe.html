Chocolate Raspberry Clusters Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of fresh Raspberries
  • 1 1/2 cup dairy free vanilla or plain yogurt
  • 1/3 cup raw cashew or almond butter
  • 9 oz chocolate chips
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil

Steps to Prepare

  1. Wash and clean raspberries, then let them dry completely.
  2. Mix nut butter and yogurt, fold in raspberries until coated completely.
  3. Using a spoon, scoop out some of the berries and yogurt and place onto a parchment paper lined baking sheet.
  4. Form the berries and yogurt into 6-8 clusters.
  5. Place clusters in the freezer for 30 mins or until frozen.
  6. In a bowl, melt the chocolate and coconut oil together using the microwave or a double boiler.
  7. Take the clusters out of the freezer and dip each one in the melted chocolate.
  8. Place them back into the sheet pan and allow the chocolate to harden.
  9. Enjoy these clusters frozen or let them thaw in the fridge for 30 minutes before enjoying.

Optional Toppings or variations

  • You could use sea salt flakes and add them on top of the cluster.
  • There are lots of chocolate chips, feel free to use depending on your taste! (dark, milk, white, semisweet)
  • Substitute the raspberries with another type of berrie like blueberries, strawberries, blackberries, etc.
  • Add sprinkles or melted drizzle.
